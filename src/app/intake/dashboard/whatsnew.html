What's New!

Happy New Year! We look forward to helping you continue to BUILD, MANAGE and GROW your practice in 2018…

    Tasks: You can now filter in Global Tasks by ‘Assigned To’ and ‘Assigned By’.
    Reports: In the Settlement Report, we have added the ability to filter by multiple statuses.
    Matter List: Matters can be sorted by Ascending/Descending File Number.
    eFax:
    • If you subscribe to a third-party eFax service you can now send faxes from CloudLex.
    • The Subscribing Managing Partner needs to turn on eFax in Settings under the Configuration Tab.
    Medical Information: We have added the ability to sort by Medical Provider and Physicians in the Medical Information tab in Details.
    Contacts:
    • In Global Contacts, there is now a Role View in addition to the Directory View.
    • In ‘Role View’ you can select to view certain Roles and Contact types.
