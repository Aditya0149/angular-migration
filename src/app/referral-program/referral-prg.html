How does the CloudLex Referral Program work?

1

Click on ‘Refer Now’ and enter your colleague’s contact details. You may enter more than one referral by re-submitting the form.

2

Your colleague will get an email and our team will connect with
them.

3

Your firm will be rewarded for all completed demos and sign-ups.

Terms & Conditions
  • The CloudLex Referral Program allows existing clients to earn promotional credits toward their CloudLex subscription by referring fellow personal injury firms to become new CloudLex clients.
  • To be eligible for the offer: All demos must be scheduled within one month of the referral date. Referred firms must sign up for CloudLex within 3 months of their demo date.
  • Your Referral Credits will be applied to your CloudLex subscription.
  • Credits are non-transferable, non-assignable, and cannot be redeemed or exchanged for cash, check, debit or credit cards.
  • In case if different employees of a firm refer the same firm, then it will be considered as one firm referral only.
  • Referrers will be notified by email when credits have been earned.
  • Credits will be applied to your upcoming CloudLex subscription payment.
  • Limited time offer. CloudLex reserves the right to terminate and/or modify this offer at any time without prior notice.
  • Referrals should only be used for personal and non-commercial purposes. Referral links should not be published or distributed on commercial websites or on blogs. Users are prohibited from “spamming” anyone with referral invitations.
