Intake Manager
Intake Manager guides you through the entire intake process (e.g., contact info, case details, injury checklists)—ensuring you capture all the information you need to successfully grow your firm. You can view important lead info (alongside vital firm data) and schedule follow-ups or reminders right from a prospect’s profile—so you never miss out on potential clients or revenue. We’ll even provide you with detailed marketing reports on which of your firm’s campaigns are bringing in the most leads, conversions, and ROI so you can budget prudently and maximize profits.
