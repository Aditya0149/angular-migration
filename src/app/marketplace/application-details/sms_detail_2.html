Expense Manager
With Expense Manager, you can make sure your obligations are always accounted for. Instead of engaging in time-consuming back and forth with your bookkeeper, your staff can simply add their matter-specific expenses to our app--where they will be tracked and organized for whoever pays the bills. From there, authorized users can record outstanding liabilities and sync them with Quickbooks--ensuring that your upcoming expenses get paid on time.
