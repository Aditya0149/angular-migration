Outlook web plugin setup

Once logged into Outlook, click on the gear icon and select “Manage add-ins” from the “Mail settings” menu.

Select "Add from URL…" from the drop-down menu and enter the URL: "https://clodulexqadocs.blob.core.windows.net/outlook-plugin/OutlookWebAddIn.xml"  

When prompted, click “Install”. Once installed, CloudLex will show up as a custom add-in under “my add-ins”.

To save an Outlook email as a note in CloudLex:
After you have received/sent an email, click on the CloudLex icon in the reading pane and enter your user information just like you’re logging into CloudLex.

Choose appropriate matter or intake from the drop-down menu and click “Upload”.

If you want to mark the Note as “Important”, select the check box.
You can also upload the attachment(s) by simply clicking on “Choose Files” and upload the files. To finalize the process, click “Upload”.

You can also add an event from your Outlook calendar to CloudLex.
To do so- go to your Outlook Calendar, select to open the appropriate “event” and click on the horizontal ellipsis "..." in the menu. Select “CloudLex” to proceed.

Select the Matter or Intake name that corresponds to the event you want to save in the CloudLex, and then click "Save".

You can see the saved event in your CloudLex account under the “Events” tab in that particular Matter or Intake.
